I am an economist studying topics in labor, personnel, and organizational economics. I am particularly interested in studying how organizations can better align talent management practices with labor market outcomes, ensuring greater equity in access to opportunities and promoting upward mobility for underrepresented groups. My work has been highlighted by prominent figures such as Steven Pinker, Raghuram Rajan, and featured in popular outlets like Marginal Revolution.
