Soumitra Shukla

Research Fellow, Harvard Business School

I am thrilled to be a Research Fellow at Harvard Business School, working at the Digital Reskilling Lab. I simultaneously work as a consultant at The Burning Glass Institute, the leading non-profit organization shaping the dialogue on the future of work.


I am particularly interested in studying how organizations can better align talent management practices with labor market outcomes, ensuring greater equity in access to opportunities and promoting upward mobility for underrepresented groups.


At HBS, I am collaborating with Raffaella Sadun and Jorge Tamayo, who co-lead the Digital Reskilling Lab. Together, we are studying the organizational processes of new technology adoption, including AI implementation and transformation in the workplace, building on the lab's core mission of studying large-scale digital training investments in private and public sector organizations.


My work has been highlighted by prominent figures such as Steven Pinker, Raghuram Rajan, and featured in popular outlets like Marginal Revolution and The Great Gender Divergence
